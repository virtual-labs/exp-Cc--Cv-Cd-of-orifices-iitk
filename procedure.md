<b>Requirements (Instruments, Chemicals & Other) : </b> <br>
•	Orifice Meter  <br>
•	Piping System  <br>
•	Supply Pump Set  <br>
•	Measuring Tank  <br>
•	Differential Manometer  <br>
•	Sump Tank <br>
•	Stop Watch <br>

<b>Procedure : </b><br>
1.	Ensure valves of inlet pipe (main valve), Orifice meter pipe line and manometer are closed. <br>
2.	Check whether the water level in the main tank is sufficient for the suction pipe of pump to be completely immersed.<br>
3.	The main valve of the pipeline selected for the experimentation is opened. <br>
4.	The ball valves of the corresponding manometer & Orifice meter are opened. <br>
5.	Now switch on the pump.<br>
6.	Adjust the control valve kept at the exit side of the Orifice to a desired flow rate and maintain the flow. <br>
7.	Wait till the flow attains a steady state.<br>
8.	Note down the readings of manometer & time for 10cm rise in measuring tank.<br>
